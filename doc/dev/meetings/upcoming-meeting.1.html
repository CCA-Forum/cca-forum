Upcoming Meeting

by damevski — last modified Oct 20, 2008 06:47 PM

Chicago, IL -- October 21-22, 2008 (Tuesday - Wednesday)

Location: Millennium Knickerbocker Hotel
Hosts: Lois Curfman McInnes and Boyana Norris, Argonne National Laboratory
Restaurants nearby: Restaurant Map 

 

Remote Participation: A telecon and MeetingPlace will be provided for anyone who wants to participate in the meeting remotely. Please sign up for the phone mailing list if you want to attend via telecon.

Agenda:

  • 8am Welcome and Where are the Bathrooms
    Lois Curfman McInnes and Boyana Norris

8am - 9am Working Breakfast
8:45am MeetingPlace session begins

  • 9am Introductions
    Rob Armstrong, SNL
  • The Next CCA Meeting in the San Francisco
    Next Host: Rob Armstrong
  • Site Selection and Scheduling for the Meeting One Year from Now
    Rob Armstrong, SNL
  • TASCS News
    David Bernholdt, Lois McInnes, Gary Kumfert, Rob Armstrong
    A brief summary of what's been going on (technically) in TASCS since the last CCA Forum meeting.
  • Event Service: Spec & Status
    Kosta Damevski (Virginia State U)
  • New Features in Babel 1.4.0
    Tom Epperly (LLNL)
  • MPIService: Spec & Status Ben Allan (SNL)
    Summary: Proposed SIDL and ccaffeine-free use cases for getting at framework-scoped MPI communicator handles will be presented.
  • Toolkit Automation Reprise Rob Armstrong (SNL)
    An open-ended discussion on the extent of download and install automation possible for toolkit components.
