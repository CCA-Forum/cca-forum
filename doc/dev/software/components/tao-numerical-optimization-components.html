Toolkit for Advanced Optimization (TAO) in CCA component form.

Name: TAO CCA Components

Author: Lois Curfman-McInnes

Description:

TAO is a suite of components that provide the TAO optimisation toolkit and is part of of the TOPS center.   Currently parts of it are in use in CCA's quantum chemistry SAP.
