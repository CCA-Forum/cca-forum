CCA Review Board (CCARB)

by kumfert — last modified Apr 14, 2010 02:36 PM

The CCA Review Board oversees changes in the CCA Specification

Introduction

The Common Component Architecture Review Board (CCARB) is nominated by the CCA Chair to oversee the development and consistency of the CCA specification.

The CCARB holds monthly teleconferences, usually on the 1st Monday of every month at 1pm USA Eastern/10 am USA Pacific time. Inquiries can be sent to ccarb at cca-forum dot org.
