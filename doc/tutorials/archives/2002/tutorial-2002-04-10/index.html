CCA Tutorial

2002-04-10 at the CCA Forum Spring Meeting

On 2002-04-10 at the Valley View Lodge, Townsend, TN, we presented our "regular" half-day tutorial prior to the CCA Forum meeting as well as a condensed "mini-tutorial" for the workshop on Adaptive Mesh Refinment co-sponsored by the CCA Forum and the Algorithmic and Software Framework for Applied Partial Differential Equations (APDEC) Center. PDF files are 2-up handout versions, rendered in greyscale.
