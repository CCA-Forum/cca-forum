ObtainingTheNeoFramework

downloading neo
At present neo is not part of the CCA-Tools distribution from Rob Armstrong.
We hope to remedy this soon.

The neo CCA binding is available from CVS to those with cca-forum accounts:
cvs -d cca-forum.org:/cvs/cca-spec/repository co cca-spec-neo

A corresponding version of Ccaffeine can usually be found in CVS:
cvs -d cca-forum.org:/cvs/ccaffeine/repository co dccafe
