Membership and Voting

The CCA Forum is a group of researchers interested in developing and advancing a standard component architecture for high performance computing. All are welcome to participate, either in quarterly meetings or email discussion groups.

Web Voting

After attendance at two consecutive quarterly meetings, one automatically becomes an official member of the CCA Forum and is eligible to vote (via the web) on motions before the forum, such as component interface specifications.
