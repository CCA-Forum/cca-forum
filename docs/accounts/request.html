CCA Forum Resource Group Request

Follow the procedure below to request membership in resource groups for the CCA Forum's collaboration resources and services. An XCAMS account is required. You can return to this page at any time to request additional groups.

  1. Select desired groups and if you had access to the old server (hosted at LBL), please provide the requested usernames to help us associate your new account with your old data.
    2. Group Purpose Your Old cca-forum.org Usernames
    CCA Web Access to authenticated web services Wiki:
    CCA Tech Access to CVS, SVN repositories, ssh logins SSH:
    TASCS Member TASCS SciDAC project resources

  2. Please provide the name of an established CCA Forum member with whom you are collaborating, or a brief explanation of why you are requesting access to these resources.

  3. When you submit this form, you will be required to authenticate your request by entering your XCAMS username and password, and possibly to synchronize your password so that XCAMS can process your group memmbership request. Your request is not complete until you see the XCAMS completion message!

  4. You will be notified by email when your request is approved.
