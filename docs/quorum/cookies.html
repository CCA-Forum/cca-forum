Quorum Cookie and Privacy Policy

The Quorum web server uses non-persistent session cookies to track user login sessions. These cookies disappear after you close your browser. Sessions time out after thirty minutes of inactivity.

Session cookies are used to maintain session state and verify access permissions to the repository. If you have any concerns about the use of non-persistent session cookies or your privacy, please contact the components team.

Any or all uses of this system and all files on this system may be intercepted, monitored, recorded, copied, audited, inspected, and disclosed to authorized Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory personnel, Department of Energy personnel, law enforcement personnel, and officials of other agencies, both domestic and foreign. By using this system, the user consents to such interception, monitoring, recording, copying, auditing, inspection, and disclosure at the discretion of the authorized Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory and Department of Energy personnel.

Improper use of this system may result in the loss of access.

These policies are specific to the Quorum WWW service and are amendments to the general disclaimers and privacy policies for Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory.
