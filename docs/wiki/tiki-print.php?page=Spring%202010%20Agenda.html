The Future of CCA Forum Meetings
David Bernholdt, ORNL

Time and budget constraints increasingly affect people's ability to attend our traditional quarterly face to face CCA Forum meetings. While remote attendance (phone and web case) has been an option for some years now, and is used by at least a few people at every Forum meeting, I think it is time to open the question of whether the Forum should change its meeting schedule or format. A few possibilities to consider include:
  • Reducing the frequency of meetings
  • Switching to a completely online format for some or all meetings
  • Some combination of the above
